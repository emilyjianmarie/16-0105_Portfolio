MAPLE WATER PACKAGING
LOGO & PACKAGING FOR MAPLE SOLUTIONS // ILLUSTRATOR, PHOTOSHOP
Maple Solutions is a company based in Canada, extracting the purest form of water from the sap of maple trees. Focusing on natural ingredients, Maple Solutions strives to put the best elements in your body for optimal hydration.

From pencil to Illustrator, Design Aesthetics required our class to experiment with leaf ideations. Starting from a maple leaf found in nature, sketching, tracing, and transforming it through different steps, a series of modules were the final product. By altering different parts of the digitalized form, I created a unique composition which translates well into a brand.
