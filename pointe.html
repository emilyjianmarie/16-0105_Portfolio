ON POINTE LOGO
LOGO FOR NEW HOME INSPECTION BUSINESS // ILLUSTRATOR, PHOTOSHOP
Bill Edie needed a logo for his start-up home inspection business located in Dover, Ohio. He wanted elements of a house to be included in the logo, expressing the purpose of his company within a few seconds. Edie requested the main colors to be royal blue and grey, contrasting against different color backgrounds. After scanning my sketches into illustrator, I experimented with different variations and arrangements of the type, creating a unique logo to be used for Edie's On Pointe Inspection brand.
