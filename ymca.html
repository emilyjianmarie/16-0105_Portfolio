POCKET COACH APP
YMCA APP FOR GYMNASTS & INSTRUCTORS // PHOTOSHOP, IPHONE 6 CAMERA
As a former gymnast and coach, I have experienced being instructed along with giving the instruction at the YMCA Gymnastics Center. While competing as a gymnast, my teammates and I loved feedback from coaches. As a coach, giving advice or teaching skills was equally rewarding.

I designed an app allowing gymnasts from every level to interact with coaches, using smartphone's video and camera capabilities. Gymnasts can upload videos of them practicing skills at home, submitting them to a YMCA coach to review and offer feedback. Coaches constructive criticism enables gymnasts to practice safely on their own. By creating a safe platform for coaches and gymnasts to connect, Pocket Coach extends practice beyond the confines of a gym.
