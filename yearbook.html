VUTAKAKA YEARBOOK
PUBLISHED VUTAKAKA YEARBOOK // INDESIGN, PHOTOSHOP
The East African Center is a non-profit organization providing education, medical care, and support to over ten thousand individuals in Eastern Kenya. They consider themselves the bridge between higher and lower income countries, fostering participation through volunteers and human interaction.

EAC currently supports a school in Takaungu, Kenya. They wanted to create a gift to congratulate their first class of graduating 8th graders, so the Executive Director of EAC reached out to me and my high school's yearbook staff. As the design editor, I brainstormed spreads for the non-profit yearbook. By incorporating traditional patterns made and sold by the locals in the village, the yearbook has a quality of personalization, unique to Takaungu, Kenya.
