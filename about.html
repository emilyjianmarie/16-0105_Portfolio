EMILY FINLEY
Graphic Communication Design // Cincinnati, Ohio
Hi, my name is Emily Finley and I am currently a Sophomore at the University of Cincinnati's College of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning. I am studying Graphic Communication Design, participating in the Professional Practice Program with alternating semesters of study with work in the field of design.

I have always had a passion for digital art, experimenting with my mother's subscription of Photoshop Elements as a child, arranging to meet a Disney Graphic Designer on family vacation, attending several design camps on summer breaks, and completing a three year interactive media course during high school. Throughout my experience with design, I have learned more about the purpose and meaning behind logos, lines of codes, and magazine layouts. Design is an outlet to making the world a more functional and innovative place, and I am looking forward to learning and contributing my skills to the industry.
